ANAGANAGA MOVIE

  • Anaganaga(transl. Once upon a time) is a 2025 Indian Telugu-language family drama film written and directed by Sunny Sanjay.[1] The film stars Sumanth, Kajal Choudhary, and Viharsh in the lead roles.[2] It is loosely adapted from the Marathi film Ekda Kaay Zala.

  • The film was released directly on the OTT platform ETV Win on 15 May 2025.[3]

    • Triler

  • "Anaganaga"tells the story of Vyas, a school teacher who champions storytelling as a teaching method, clashing with the conventional, exam-focused approach of the school where he works and where his wife is the principal.
